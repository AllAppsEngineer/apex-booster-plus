## Long description

Apex Booster + is the gaming prep app that transforms your routine before you play.

Organize your game library, analyze your device's real status, load your preference
profile, and start every session with focus, style, and control.

── WHAT APEX BOOSTER + DOES ──

▸ Personal game library
Add your favorite games directly from your installed apps. Favorite, organize, and
quickly access any game in your collection.

▸ Apex Scan — Local device diagnostic
Check available RAM status and app-measured latency before your session. Real data,
read directly from your own device.

▸ GFX profile per game
Save a visual preference per game — Balanced, Performance, Quality, or Battery Saver.
Your profile is stored locally and loaded automatically next session.

▸ Apex Boost Mode
Activate the Apex prep sequence before entering your game. A cinematic visual experience
that loads your profile and opens the game with style.

▸ Session history
Every session is recorded automatically. Track when you played, which profile was active,
and what local data was available.

▸ Gamer Focus Mode
Enable full focus before playing with Android Do Not Disturb integration. Opt-in and
reversible at any time.

▸ Three language support
Português, English and Español — switchable directly in settings.

── HONEST PREPARATION ──

Apex Booster + does not modify third-party games, does not alter CPU, GPU, or internal
resolution, does not automatically clear RAM, and does not promise ping reduction or
FPS increase. The app prepares your session, organizes your library, and displays real
data from your own device.

No ads. No subscription. Just prep, focus, and play.

Prepare. Scan. Play.
